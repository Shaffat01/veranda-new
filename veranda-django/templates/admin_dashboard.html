About Veranda

Modern, welcoming event venue designed for gatherings that matter

Our Story

Veranda is a modern, welcoming event venue designed for gatherings that matter. Inspired by the warmth of a veranda, a place where people naturally come together, our space brings comfort, connection, and celebration under one roof.

With a flexible ballroom, private entrance, outdoor terrace with firepits, and seamless access to Spark by Hilton Bensalem, Veranda blends contemporary style with heartfelt hospitality. From weddings and cultural celebrations to milestone events and corporate functions, we provide an elevated, adaptable setting where every occasion feels genuinely welcomed.

At Veranda, our brand is built on simplicity, sincerity, and service. We offer a beautiful space, a supportive team, and the freedom to create an event that feels like you.
